Them weapon moi:
 - Tao weapon o player ( object weapon o ban tay phai)
 - Gan script Weapon, AudioSource vao weapon moi, gan them script MeeleWeaponTrail neu la kiem
 - Neu la sung thi them object firePoint vao Weapon, neu la kiem thi them Object DealDamageTrigger va Tip
 - Them weaponId o PlayerAttacking.cs (Trong ham ChangeWeapon)
 - Them weapon skill o Weapon.cs neu muon weapon co skill rieng
 - Tao prefab Item de hien tren bag cho weapon theo duong dan "Resources\Prefabs\Items\Weapon", thay doi cac thong so trong ItemScript dac biet la WeaponID
 - Them currenWeaponId trong ham ButtonEquipItem cua script ItemManagerAction.cs
 - Tao prefab Loot de co the loot weapon theo duong dan "Resources\Prefabs\Loot\Weapons", thay doi item to add trong collider2d
 - Neu weapon co the craft duoc thi thuc hien cac buoc tiep theo
 - Vao Scene Home -> UICanvas -> CraftPanel/ExchangePanel -> CraftList -> ViewPort -> ItemManager, tạo item craft ( copy lai tu cai co roi, doi ten cho dung voi weapon, doi hinh va doi requiment item)
